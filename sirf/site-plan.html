Overview

Purpose

The website will convey the fun that people of all ages can experience being a guest of SIRF and taking part in all or some engaging diverse activities during the four days of the street art cultural event witha great 35-year-old history. The site will advertise SIRF as multicultural event that shouldn't be missed by anyone because it is worth to take place in any capital of the world.

Audience

Target audience:
all people because SIRF provides different types of performances and activities,
but mainly families with children and young adults interested in modern types of dance and music.
